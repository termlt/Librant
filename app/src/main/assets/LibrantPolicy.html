Privacy Policy

Last Update Date: June 12, 2024

Welcome to Librant! This Privacy Policy describes how Librant ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use Librant.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from users who provide their consent:

  • Geolocation: To show the route visualization from your home to the book owner's home.
  • Phone Number: To facilitate contact between users regarding book sharing.
  • Name and Surname: To identify you within the app and to other users.
  • Email Address: To create and manage your account.
  • Google Account Information: If you choose to sign up via your Google account, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • Camera: With your permission, we may access your camera to allow you to take photos within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Facilitate communication and book sharing between users.
  • Personalize your experience within the app.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users and third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our app is available to users of all ages. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Your Choices

You can manage your account information and communication preferences at any time through the app settings. You can also delete your account by contacting us at lastnovel@protonmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
